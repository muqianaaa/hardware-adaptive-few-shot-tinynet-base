# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want this project to be welcoming, constructive, and respectful for everyone.

Contributors are expected to participate in good faith, communicate professionally,
and focus on improving the code, documentation, and reproducibility of the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent and give actionable, technical feedback.
- Keep collaboration focused on the project and its users.
- Share context clearly when reporting bugs, regressions, or hardware issues.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project discussions, issues, pull requests, and
other community interactions related to this repository.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions
that do not align with this code of conduct.
